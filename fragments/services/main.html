Community Health Workers

We have a team of Community Health Workers who can connect individuals with sickle cell disease and their families to the resources they need

Healthcare Providers

We identify healthcare providers in the state of Arizona who specialize in the treatment of sickle cell disease

Support Groups

We host quarterly Zoom meetings providing an opportunity for learning and sharing experiences

Events

We plan a variety of events throughout the year to promote learning or to simply allow families to enjoy time together

Financial Assistance

We provide financial assistance for individuals with sickle cell disease and their families

Educational Grants

We offer grants to individuals who are obtaining higher level education

360 SCD Hub

We provide a mobile and web app for tracking personal health care information, learning about sickle cell disease and more
